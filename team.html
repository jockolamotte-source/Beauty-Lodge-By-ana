Meet Our Team

Meet the people behind Beauty Lodge by Anna and the work that goes into creating a calm, personal, and welcoming experience for every guest.

01

CEO · Owner

Anna

Owner, Licensed Esthetician & Permanent Jewelry Specialist

Originally from Kharkiv, Ukraine, Anna moved to the United States in 2017 and later graduated from Rudae’s School of Beauty Culture in Fort Wayne, Indiana. She is a licensed esthetician with a passion for creating beauty services that feel personal, polished, and thoughtfully tailored to each client.

Through custom lashes, permanent makeup, and other beauty treatments, Anna focuses on enhancing natural features rather than changing them. Her approach is warm, precise, and detail-oriented, with every appointment designed to feel relaxed, unrushed, and centered around the client.

Beauty Lodge reflects Anna’s belief that beauty should feel effortless and confidence-building. She takes the time to understand each guest’s style and goals, then creates a look that feels natural, flattering, and uniquely theirs.

Anna married the love of her life, Jake, in 2025 and is a proud stepmom to Lucy. Family is at the heart of everything she does, and outside the studio she loves spending time with them, caring for her pets, finding a great cup of coffee, enjoying the outdoors, and taking on creative DIY projects around the home. She brings that same warmth, creativity, and attention to detail into her work, making every guest feel comfortable, cared for, and beautiful.
